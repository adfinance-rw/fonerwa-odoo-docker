About This Module

This module applies the official Rwanda Green Fund brand colors throughout the entire Odoo interface, ensuring a consistent and professional look that reflects RGF's commitment to environmental sustainability and growth.

The color palette incorporates earthy tones that convey a sense of sustainability, environmental consciousness, and a connection to nature.
